BayesPI -- supporting data, methods and programs

Supporting Results 1: predicted TF energy matrices of 61 yeast TFs in YPD condition by using ChIP-chip data (Harbison CT et al 2004. Nature 431).

Supporting Results 2: predicted TF energy matrices of yeast TFs under stress conditions by using ChIP-chip data (Harbison CT et al 2004. Nature 431).

Supporting Results 3: predicted TF energy matrices in three yeast species by using ChIP tiling array data (Bomeman AR et al 2007. Science 317).

Supporting Results 4: predicted TF energy matrices of 10 yeast TFs by using nucleosome positioning information (Lee CK et al 2004 Nat Genet 36).

Supporting Results 5: predicted TF energy matrices of three human TFs by using ChIP-Seq data (Jothi R et al 2008. Nucleic acids research 36).
